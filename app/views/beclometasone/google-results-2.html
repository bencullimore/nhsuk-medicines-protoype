Beclometasone dipropionate

Medicine

Beclometasone, also known as beclometasone dipropionate, and sold under the brand name Qvar among others, is a steroid medication. It is available as an inhaler, cream, pills, and nasal spray. The inhaled form is used in the long-term management of asthma. The cream may be used for dermatitis and psoriasis.

Molar mass: 521.042 g/mol

CAS ID: 4419-39-0

Formula: C28H37ClO7

Trade name: Qvar, Beconase AQ, others

Elimination half-life: 2.8 hours

ATC code: A07EA07 (WHO) D07AC15 (WHO), R01AD01 (WHO), R03BA01 (WHO)
